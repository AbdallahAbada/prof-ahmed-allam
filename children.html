Tonsillectomy

A surgical procedure to remove the tonsils, often used to treat recurrent tonsillitis, sleep-disordered breathing, or other related conditions.

Adenoidectomy

A surgical procedure to remove the adenoids, typically performed to treat recurrent infections, chronic nasal congestion, or other related conditions.

Grommets (ventilation tubes)

A surgical procedure to insert tiny tubes into the eardrum to help drain fluid and improve hearing, often used to treat chronic middle ear infections or other related conditions.

Cholesteatoma surgery

A surgical procedure to remove a cholesteatoma, which is a type of benign growth in the middle ear that can cause hearing loss, balance problems, and other related symptoms.

Complex ear surgery

A broad term used to describe a range of surgical procedures to treat complex ear conditions, including reconstruction of the ear canal, repair of the eardrum, and removal of tumors or other growths in the ear.

Chronic ear and sinus infections

A range of treatments and interventions to help manage chronic ear and sinus infections, which can cause recurring symptoms such as pain, pressure, congestion, and drainage.
