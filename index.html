Meet Prof. Ahmed Allam

A Highly Trained and Experienced ENT Specialist.

Prof. Ahmed Allam is a highly experienced ENT specialist, trained in dr and England. With over 15 years of experience, he has performed 4,000 ENT surgeries, specializing in ear, sinus, and skull base procedures. Prof. Allam holds a master's degree in endoscopic endonasal approaches to the skull base and a PhD in cochlear implantation. He is currently a consultant at Queens University Hospitals in London, where he provides high-quality care in otology, children's surgery, and sinus surgery. Prof. Allam is also actively involved in supervising and mentoring trainees.

Children Ent Treatment

I understand the importance of providing gentle and effective treatments for children. I offer a range of services to treat conditions such as tonsillectomy, adenoids, grommets, cholesteatoma surgery, chronic ear and sinus infections, and more. We strive to make your child's experience as comfortable and stress-free as possible, with a welcoming and kid-friendly environment.

Adults Ent Treatment

My clinics in London & Egypt offer a range of treatments to help adults manage their ENT conditions effectively. I specialize in treatments such as balloon dilatation of the eustachian tube, balloon sinuplasty, coblation tonsillectomy, endoscopic ear surgery, septoplasty surgery, sinus surgery (functional endoscopic sinus surgery or FESS), voice surgery, neck lumps, tonsillectomy for adults, intratympanic injections.
